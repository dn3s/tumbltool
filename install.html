Installing

A better system will be figured out at some point. Until then, it's still pretty easy. Checkout the Github repo. As long as you have the dependencies installed, you can try it out right as it is before installing.

You will need Perl 5, as well as the JSON, CSS::Minify, JavaScript::Minify, HTML::Entities, HTML::Escape, and URI::Escape modules from CPAN (your distribution should also provide packages).

Put the TumblTool directory somewhere in your Perl installation's @INC path, put the tumbltool executable somewhere in your shell's $PATH, and rename the example_content directory either to /etc/tumbltool or $HOME/.tumbltool (or just keep it somewhere else and specify its location on the CLI, but that's messy).
