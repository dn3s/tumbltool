TumblTool

A Better Workflow for Tumblr Themes

This project was born of frustration with Tumblr's theme editor. I had a lot of theme ideas and I wanted to put them out quickly and easily. The goal of this project is to bring a much-needed dose of sanity to the Tumblr theme development workflow.

TumblTool is written by dn3s, also known as Devon Sawatzky. Bugs, issues, and feature requests can be reported in GitHub, or you can contact me at tumbltool@devonsawatzky.ca.
